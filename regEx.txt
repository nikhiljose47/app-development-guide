Whole select number/pattern
Operators: +
ex: [0-9]+ , + will match one or more of what precedes it (a number in this case)

Replace
(content) -> $1
ex: 
Problem: Make = 1-664 --> (+1-664)
regEx: ([0-9|-]+$)  --> (+$1)

Usage in regEx 
regex = new Regex("^[0-9]+$");
The ^ will anchor the beginning of the string, 
the $ will anchor the end of the string,
the + will match one or more of what precedes it (a number in this case)
